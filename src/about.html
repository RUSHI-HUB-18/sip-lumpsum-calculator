About Rushi Finance Tools

Professional-grade financial calculators, built with care for everyone

Our Mission

Rushi Finance Tools was created with a simple goal — to make professional-grade financial planning accessible to everyone. Whether you're planning your first SIP, calculating EMI for a home loan, or preparing for retirement, our tools give you accurate, visual, and easy-to-understand results.

All calculations use industry-standard financial formulas. The platform is 100% free, runs entirely in your browser, and never sends your data to any server. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

📈
Accurate Calculations
All formulas follow RBI and SEBI standards. SIP, Lumpsum, EMI — every result is financially precise.
🔒
100% Private
All data stays in your browser's localStorage. No server, no tracking, no data collection.
📱
Mobile-First Design
Optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops. Beautiful UI with dark mode support.
🤖
AI-Powered
Natural language investment assistant. Just type your goal and get instant results.
📊
Visual Charts
Interactive doughnut and line charts powered by Chart.js for clear wealth visualization.
💾
Save & Export
Save calculations to your account, export as CSV, share on WhatsApp & Twitter.
